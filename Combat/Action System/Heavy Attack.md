## Purpose
A **Heavy [[Attacks|Attack]]** represents a more powerful, committed strike that trades [[Speed & Resolution|speed]] or flexibility for increased [[Damage Types|damage]]. It is ideal for breaking through defences or dealing significant harm to a single target.

## Mechanics
- **[[Actions|Action]] Cost:** 2 full [[actions]]  
  - Perform a melee or ranged [[Attacks|Attack]] that deals **double [[Damage Types|damage]]**.
  - Reduces speed by -2.
  - Apply any standard modifiers ([[Attributes|Might]], Weapon bonuses, Critical Hits) as usual.
