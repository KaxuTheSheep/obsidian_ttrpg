### Overview
The Nobles of Solasfall are the city’s masked power brokers — wealthy, influential, and deeply entwined in governance that touches every facet of life. While they serve the King in name, most understand that true authority lies with the Royal Advisors. Behind carefully maintained facades of refinement and tradition, they engage in subtle wars of influence, loyalty, and betrayal.

### Role & Influence
- Nobles govern vast estates and dominate political offices, particularly within the Noble District.
- Act as patrons of the arts, magic, trade, and military orders, seeking to expand power and legacy.
- Many align secretly with the Royal Advisors or plot against them, leveraging influence within the bureaucracy, tax collection, law enforcement, and administration.

### Internal Politics & Rivalries
- Fractured into competing houses and families, each with unique ambitions, alliances, and vendettas.
- Tools of power include espionage, arranged marriages, and assassination.
- Some houses maintain clandestine ties to The Thieves' Guild, Cultists, or The Rebel Group for leverage.

### Culture and Expectations
- Publicly embody education, decorum, and controlled displays of power.
- Patronage of institutions like the Magic Institutions and The Knights Orders is a status symbol.
- Behind closed doors, family honour, legacy, and tradition influence decisions, though pragmatism often prevails.
- Many nobles privately resent the King’s incapacitation and the Royal Advisors’ shadow rule but avoid open rebellion to prevent chaos.

### Public Perception
- Among common citizens, nobles inspire envy, suspicion, and resentment.
- Frequently blamed for corruption, inequality, and the burdensome The Weighing.
- Yet public works, festivals, and religious patronage help maintain a fragile goodwill.
