### Overview
Solasfall may be ruled in name by its King, but the throne has long since become a symbolic illusion — carefully maintained by those who hold true power behind veils of magic and bureaucracy. The Governance sets laws, oversees infrastructure, and mediates disputes while managing the fragile balance between factions, magical threats, and Hollowdeep expeditions.

### The King's Fate
- During the final moments of the Veil's creation, the King suffered a magical catastrophe that ruptured his mind, spirit, or body irreparably.
- Though he initially survived, his condition deteriorated silently until he entered a state of suspended consciousness — unresponsive and unreachable.
- Only the Royal Advisors know the full truth of his condition.
- The Royal Mages, some involved in the incident, either believe he recovered or avoid questioning his absence.
- To the public, the King is “in retreat,” with rare appearances sustained through illusion, proxy, or enchanted performance.

### The Royal Advisors — The True Rulers
- An inner circle of elite mages, bureaucrats, and political manipulators who shape every policy, decree, and secret of the city.
- Their exact number is unknown, though most believe there are between five and nine core Advisors.
- They command The Royal Mages, oversee the Magic Institutions, manage Rift strategy, and control all internal governance, from taxation to justice.
- Their primary goals are pragmatic: maintain control, stabilize the Veil, and extract as much from the Hollowdeep as possible.

### Rule Through Layers
- The Advisors operate through a lattice of public officials — magistrates, taxmen, stewards, judges — all reporting to unnamed “representatives.”
- They rarely appear in public, relying on magical proxies, illusions, or empowered agents.
- Some whisper they are immortal, sustained by Veil energy, or no longer entirely Human.

### Public Perception
- The common folk revere the King as a near-divine figure, unaware of the illusion.
- The nobility suspect the truth: the King no longer rules. Whether he is sick, spellbound, or withdrawn is uncertain, but all know the Advisors hold real power.
- Dissenters and theorists vanish often, usually reappearing in research wards, Rift expeditions, or not at all.

### Symbol & Motto

A stylised stone tower rising from a circular walled city, representing order and surveillance.  
_"Order above all, for the city endures."_
