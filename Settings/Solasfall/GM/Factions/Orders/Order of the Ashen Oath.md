### Overview

The Order of the Ashen Oath embodies zeal, sacrifice, and relentless devotion. They serve as Solasfall’s crusaders, purging corruption in the Hollowdeep and enforcing divine and civic law above all else. Their campaigns are both a spiritual calling and a martial necessity, blending battlefield ferocity with unwavering Faith.

They are closely tied to The Ashen Crown Faith, with many knights drawing divine strength from their devotion. The order also incorporates essence users, who draw power from the Hollowdeep, making them uniquely lethal on expeditions. Lifebinders support enhances all members, reinforcing both divine and essence-driven abilities.

### Leadership

**General Kaelen Thorne** – A commanding figure of stern discipline and quiet intensity. Kaelen balances devout Faith with tactical acumen, personally overseeing both military operations and spiritual training. Known for inspiring loyalty through example, he is relentless in battle yet patient with apprentices.

### Magic, Divinity, and Essences
- **Divinity:** Followers of the Ashen Crown gain access to divine powers. Only followers may channel divinity directly.
- **Blessings:** Any knight, regardless of personal Faith, may receive Blessings from a devout member. Power scales with the giver’s Faith.
- **Magic:** Innate and learned magic exists among the order’s members, particularly among magus knights, but is secondary to divine and Essences methods.
- **Essences:** Certain knights consume Hollowdeep Essences, gaining permanent attribute boosts or abilities. Essences coexist with Blessings, without conflict however a person with an essence cannot learn magic or use divinity.

### Training & Methods
- **Combat:** Intense martial and battlefield training. Combines swordsmanship, tactical manoeuvres, and synergistic use of Essences and divine abilities.
- **Rituals:** Daily devotions, blessing ceremonies, and purification rites. Hollowdeep expeditions begin with oaths and consecrations to the Ashen Crown.
- **Enhancements:** Lifebinders provide physical and magical augmentation, stabilizing essence powers and reinforcing divine channels. Regular infusion of mana-based healing wards ensures prolonged endurance.

### Allies & Affiliations
- **Lifebinders:** Provide physical and magical enhancements for combat and survival, as well as healing to boost training speed.
- **Ashen Crown Sanctum:** Spiritual guidance, Blessings, and ritual support.
- **Emberwright Sanctum:** Occasional collaboration for Equipment and expeditionary tools.
- **Explorers’ Guild:** Joint operations for Hollowdeep expeditions.

### Public Perception
- **Citizens:** Revered for bravery and piety; feared in war.
- **Nobility:** Respected as instruments of order; consulted in religious and military matters.
- **Explorers/Hollowdeep Expeditions:** Trusted protectors and indispensable guides.
- **Other Knight Orders:** Admired and sometimes envied for their zeal and divine favor.

### Symbol & Motto
- **Symbol:** A cracked sword wreathed in flame.
- **Motto:** “Only fire cleanses.”

### Notes
- Highly versatile, blending Essences, divine power, and magic without conflict.
- Known for purification campaigns and extreme loyalty to the Ashen Crown.
- Regularly participates in joint ventures with Lifebinders for Hollowdeep operations.
