### Overview

The **Order of the Iron Star** serves as Solasfall’s immovable bulwark — protectors of its streets, walls, and outer farmlands. They are the first and last line of defence against threats both from within and beyond the city’s bounds. Often stationed along the great gates, market squares, and rift perimeters, the Iron Star embodies the discipline and steadfastness that keep Solasfall from falling into chaos.

Their duty is less glorious than it is relentless: long watches, riot suppression, and guarding those the city depends on but rarely celebrates — its farmers, merchants, and travellers.

### Role & Duties
- Guard city exits, defensive walls, and vital infrastructure.
- Maintain peace in the lower districts, especially during unrest or Weighing disputes.
- Escort Hollowdeep expeditions to and from the surface when required.
- Serve as first responders to breaches or divine disturbances within the Veil.

### Methods & Training
- Specialise in formation warfare, riot control, and fortified combat.
- Heavy Armour and disciplined phalanx tactics define their approach — efficiency over spectacle.
- Recruits undergo rigorous endurance training and conditioning in silence and focus; speech during drills is forbidden until completion.
- While magic is uncommon, **Essence Users** and **Divine Followers** are common, with precise and non-destructive abilities preferred over destructive power.

### Faith & Allegiances
- The Order’s chapels often venerate **The Pale Bloom**, **The Verdant Hand**, or **The Candle Mother**, whose teachings of preservation and protection align with their values.
- Followers and Lifebinders occasionally accompany patrols to tend to the wounded or purify corrupted soil beyond the walls.
- The **Aetherbron Institution** maintains a respectful alliance, providing enchanted arms and warding stones for the city’s perimeter defences.

### Composition & Culture
- Predominantly Human, though among the Orders, the Iron Star is the most open to non-Human recruits.
- They value steadiness, obedience, and protection of the weak — but also despise cowardice and needless aggression.
- New initiates are assigned to week-long gate vigils as their rite of passage, standing motionless beside the gates of Solasfall until released at dawn on the seventh day.

### Public Perception
- Respected and beloved by commoners for their visible protection and humility.
- Nobles and bureaucrats often view them as simple-minded or overly rigid, despite depending on their vigilance.
- Their constancy has made them symbols of safety in Solasfall’s streets — “an Iron Star watches” is a phrase mothers tell their children when the night feels unsafe.

### Leadership
The Order of the Iron Star is commanded by its **General**, who answers directly to the King and the Royal Advisors. Traditionally, the General’s identity is shrouded in disciplined anonymity, reflecting the Order’s creed: _“No name stands above the wall.”_ Currently, the Order is led by **Temporary General Ronan Veyric**, a seasoned former field commander whose expertise in defensive operations and Hollowdeep Essences ensures the city’s walls and borders remain secure. Though his name is known within official circles, public recognition remains minimal, maintaining the Order’s culture of duty above personal glory.
