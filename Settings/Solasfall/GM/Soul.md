The soul is not a mystical or separable entity. It is an emergent phenomenon — a stable internal mana current produced by neurological activity in a living creature. While mana interacts with all matter, the soul is the specific current that arises from a brain, and it is the only thing that allows a creature to *control* mana rather than simply be affected by it.

## Origin

Every living thing with neurological activity generates a soul. The brain's electrical and chemical processes cause ambient mana particles to form a persistent internal current that runs through the nervous system. This current is not stored in any one place — it exists as a continuous flow, originating from the brain and extending outward through the nervous system into the rest of the body.

Plants and other organisms without neurological activity do not produce a soul. They are affected by mana like any other matter, but they generate no current and therefore have no conducting ability.

## The Mana Signature

A soul is not directly visible or measurable. What observers and mages perceive is the *mana signature* — the pattern of excitation states and flow characteristics that the soul's current produces in the ambient mana around it. The signature is the observable expression of the soul, not the soul itself.

A creature's signature reflects its nature: its biology, experience, instincts, and accumulated identity. No two signatures are identical. The signature is the reason Essences carry abilities and traits — when a creature dies in the Hollowdeep's dense mana environment, its signature may crystallise into physical form rather than dissipating, preserving a fragment of what the creature was.

Signature complexity scales with neurological complexity. A simple creature produces a simpler current with fewer distinct characteristics. A creature of greater neurological sophistication produces a richer, more layered signature with more potential for varied abilities. This is why Essences from more complex creatures tend to carry stronger or more varied effects.

## The Soul as Conductor

The soul's current is what allows a creature to conduct mana. A mage does not generate mana — they use their soul as a sorting mechanism, drawing in ambient particles, imposing direction and coordination, and releasing them toward an intended effect. The soul provides the structure that turns random mana flow into controlled manipulation.

This is why mana affects plants and inert matter without them being able to respond in kind. The interaction is passive — mana changes things it contacts based on its excitation state. Only a creature with a soul can actively shape what mana does.

Damaging the soul through Mana Overload does not destroy the soul but impairs the neurological structures generating the current. The signature quiets and conducting ability is lost. The creature still has a soul — they simply can no longer use it as a conduit.

## Reach and Extent

The soul's current is not limited to the brain. It extends through the nervous system into the rest of the body. This is why physical changes to the soul — such as consuming an Essence — produce physical changes in the body: the current's altered pattern propagates outward, and the body adapts to match it. Denser muscle tissue, altered physiology, new biological capabilities all arise from the body conforming to a changed current.

The brain is the source and anchor of the current. Damage to the brain disrupts the soul directly. Body parts retain their connection to the soul as long as the nervous pathway to the brain remains intact or the current can still reach them.

## Death and Dissipation

When neurological activity ceases, the current collapses. Without the brain's ongoing processes to sustain it, the soul's mana disperses back into the ambient environment. This dissipation is natural and complete under normal conditions.

In environments of exceptionally high mana density — such as the Hollowdeep — dissipation can be interrupted. The surrounding mana is dense enough that a collapsing signature may instead crystallise, preserving the pattern in physical form as an Essence. The likelihood of crystallisation depends on the density of the local mana environment, not on the creature's complexity. A simpler creature in a very dense realm may crystallise. A complex creature in a sparse area likely will not.

## Deities and the Soul

Deities are mana constructs sustained by collective belief. Belief is itself a product of neurological activity — every worshipper's brain contributes to the construct's coherence through the ambient mana their soul-current influences. Deities are, in this sense, built from the collective soul-output of their followers, even without any direct connection to individual worshippers.

A Follower's relationship is more direct. Through dedicated faith and the completion of a personal quest, a Follower's soul-current synchronises with the deity's mana construct. The current does not merge with or surrender to the deity — it touches it, forming a persistent resonant link. Divine power flows through this connection rather than from the Follower's own conducting. The soul remains the Follower's own. The deity runs alongside it, not through it.

This link is a two-way contact point. In theory, sustained synchronisation with a deity could influence a Follower's soul-current over time — which may manifest mechanically through certain class abilities, and narratively through changes in disposition, instinct, or perception.

## Essences and the Soul

Consuming an Essence merges a fragment of another creature's crystallised mana signature into the consumer's own current. The soul does not reject this — it incorporates it, and the current's altered pattern propagates through the nervous system, producing physical and mental changes in the body.

The changes reflect the absorbed signature's nature entirely, including its weaknesses. The soul is not selective. Drawbacks cannot be separated from benefits because the signature is not separable into parts — it is a whole pattern, and the body conforms to all of it.

Essences cannot be removed once absorbed. They can only be exchanged during a Long Rest, in which the old signature fragment is displaced by attunement to a new one.
