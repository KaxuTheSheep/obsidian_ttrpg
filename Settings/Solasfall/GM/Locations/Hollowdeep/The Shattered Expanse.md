### Overview:
The Shattered Expanse is the entry floor of the Hollowdeep, a surreal and disorienting landscape that mirrors itself on ceiling and floor. Grey, jagged rocks form the ground, while leafless, bone-white trees twist and reach both above and below, creating a sense of infinity. The environment is fractured — the terrain bends outward from the centre in four directions, each pointing toward a portal leading to deeper Floors.

### Structure:
- The centre of the Shattered Expanse serves as the main hub for explorers entering the Hollowdeep.
- Each portal lies at the end of a mirrored quadrant, emphasizing the floor’s unnatural geometry.
- The floor itself is largely empty of life, making the barren, mirrored landscape feel eerily quiet and vast.

### Function:
- The Shattered Expanse contains portals to four distinct Floors, The Iron Ravine, The Obsidian Glade, The Sundered Marsh and The Glass Expanse #Wip 
- Explorers must navigate the twisting, mirrored terrain carefully to reach these portals.
- Safe zones are rare, if they exist at all, making the Shattered Expanse more a testing ground than a place of rest.

### Environment Notes:
- Gravity is slightly distorted, bending perception and making the mirrored reflections more than just optical illusions.
- The floor serves as a psychological introduction to the Hollowdeep, teaching Explorers to expect disorientation and danger.
