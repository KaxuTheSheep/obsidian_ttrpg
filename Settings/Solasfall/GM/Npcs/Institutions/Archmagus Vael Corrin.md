**Official Title:** Archmagus of the Riftconjurors Institution  
**Role:** Precise and methodical master of spatial magic, specializing in rift creation, Hollowdeep expeditions, and spatial research within Solasfall. Oversees all rift-related operations.  
**Status:** Alive  
**Age:** 38  
**Condition:** Physically robust; prolonged mana manipulation sometimes causes mild vertigo or disorientation. Mentally disciplined, highly focused.  
**Race:** Velkhari  
**Languages:** Common, Veilscent, Solari

## Overview
Vael Corrin embodies quiet authority and obsessive precision. His pale, pupil-less eyes constantly measure space and rift lines, a natural extension of his Velkhari connection to the Rift. Chronologically only 38, he was already active in early Veil stabilisation projects shortly after its creation, giving him both reputation and influence beyond his apparent age.
Though socially distant, he leverages subtle intimidation from his inhuman appearance and calm demeanor to command respect. He values meticulous preparation above charisma and considers fear, awe, or misunderstanding by the public as minor tools to maintain control over rift operations. His attention to detail has prevented multiple Hollowdeep disasters and keeps Solasfall’s magical infrastructure stable.

## Appearance
Tall and lean, pale ghostly skin, pupil-less eyes reflecting Rift energy, shark-like teeth occasionally visible. Silver hair cropped short. Wears austere slate and muted silver robes, etched with faint geometric patterns that subtly shift when moving. Hands are calloused from casting and spatial etching. Staff inlaid with small rotating spheres represents stabilized rift points. His movements are deliberate and precise, radiating calm authority and latent menace.

## Traits
- **Spatial Perfectionist** – Obsessive control over rifts and spatial magic; minor errors unacceptable.
- **Methodical & Disciplined** – Procedural in both magic and institutional governance.
- **Quiet Authority** – Commands respect without overt displays of power or emotion.
- **Safety-Oriented** – Prioritises Hollowdeep expedition security and rift integrity.
- **Subtle Manipulator** – Uses precise, minimal interventions to guide outcomes without drawing attention.
- **Velkhari Advantage** – Natural affinity with Rift energy, stealth, and intimidation.
