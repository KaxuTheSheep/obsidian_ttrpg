**Official Title:** Headmaster of the Common Arcanum  
**Role:** Specialist in illusion and shadow magic, responsible for foundational training of beginner mages and preparing them for specialised institutions. Focuses on magical theory, practical spellcraft, and subtle manipulation of perception.  
**Status:** Alive  
**Age:** 47  
**Condition:** Healthy, mentally sharp, occasionally fatigued from overseeing multiple beginner cohorts.  
**Race:** Velkhari  
**Languages:** Common, Veilscent, Solari

## Faction Relationships
- **Royal Mages** – Respected mentors; relies on them for sanctioned magical guidance and coordination.
- **Knights Orders** – Values their discipline; occasionally integrates beginner shadow techniques for tactical instruction.
- **Other Magic Institutions** – Highly collegial; frequently exchanges knowledge with other headmasters.
- **The Governance of Solasfall** – Maintains professional compliance; minimal political involvement.
- **The Ministry of Balance** – Interacts only as required for institutional regulation.
- **Explorers’ Guild & Hollowdeep Expeditions** – Occasionally advises beginner expeditions; indirect collaboration.
- **Rebel Group** – Cautiously aware of their presence; minor monitoring through students.
- **Cultists** – Rarely encountered; shadow magic used for defensive precaution.

## Overview
Selene Thariel is a calm, meticulous Velkhari who balances authority with subtle encouragement. She is widely respected for her expertise in perception manipulation and shadowcraft, though her primary focus remains on the foundational education of new mages.

She nurtures talent, ensuring students are capable of adapting to the specialised demands of other Magic Institutions. Her presence exudes quiet authority, and she is often seen observing students closely, correcting mistakes with gentle but firm guidance.

## Appearance
Tall and lean, with pale reflective skin and faint Rift-hued eyes typical of Velkhari. Long, silvery hair flows behind her robes, which are dark with subtle shifting patterns mimicking shadow effects. Her movements are deliberate, calm, and precise; her hands often trace arcane gestures even when idle.

## Traits
- **Perception Manipulator:** Exceptional control over illusions and shadows.
- **Mentor & Educator:** Patient, disciplined, and attentive to beginner skill development.
- **Quiet Authority:** Commands respect through calm presence and expertise rather than force.
- **Cross-Institution Collaborator:** Maintains strong ties with other headmasters for novice training.
- **Strategic Observer:** Uses subtle assessments to identify potential and prevent magical mishaps.
