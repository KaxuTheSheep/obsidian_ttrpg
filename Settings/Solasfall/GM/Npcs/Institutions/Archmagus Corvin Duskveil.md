## Overview
Corvin Duskveil embodies both mastery and menace. His Stitched form, marked with subtle seams and faintly glowing eyes, immediately signals otherness, feeding public unease. Yet within the Prismshapers Institution, his meticulous training, precise execution of shadowcraft, and ethical pragmatism command respect from faculty and students alike.

He balances fear and respect carefully: outwardly, a professional, disciplined headmaster who treats students and colleagues with formal courtesy; privately, his presence and reputation project a quiet intimidation, reminding all that underestimation of a Stitched master is dangerous. His approach is shaped by decades of societal prejudice, crafting a personality both cautious and commanding.

Corvin frequently participates in Hollowdeep missions requiring discretion, environmental manipulation, or psychological control. His Stitched resilience and patchwork form make him unnervingly effective in such roles, able to withstand injuries or stress that would incapacitate others.

## Appearance
Corvin is medium height with a perfectly symmetrical, older Stitched form — seams are present but subtly reinforced, and his pale, patchworked skin shows faint variations from prior lives. Eyes glow faintly amber, reflecting residual vitality and mana. He wears layered, dark robes with muted arcane sigils, practical for both teaching and fieldwork. Hands are reinforced at seams, allowing precise somatic spellwork. His movement is deliberate and measured, projecting calm authority and quiet menace.
