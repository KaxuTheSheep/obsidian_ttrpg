**Environment:** A jagged chasm of rust-colored rock and flowing molten streams, with precarious ledges and unstable terrain. Gravity shifts in small patches, creating sudden drops or floating boulders.  
**Hazards & Challenges:** Lava fissures, collapsing bridges, intense heat zones. Traversing safely requires agility and careful observation.  
**Portals:** Leads deeper into Hollowdeep; also connects to Floor Two B and Floor Two C via hidden or arcane passages.
