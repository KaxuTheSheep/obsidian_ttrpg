### Overview
The Royal Mages are Solasfall’s elite corps of magical experts, tasked with maintaining the Veil, overseeing Riftconjurors Institution operations, and enforcing magical law when needed. While nominally serving the King and the Royal Advisors, they maintain a degree of autonomy in both research and tactical deployment. They blend scholarly pursuit with disciplined combat capability, projecting both awe and fear throughout the city.

### Role & Influence
- Responsible for sustaining the Veil’s mana, ensuring citywide stability and environmental control.
- Supervise rift operations and Hollowdeep expeditions in collaboration with Explorers’ Guild and Riftconjurors Institution.
- Serve as a magical enforcement arm for Knights Orders, occasionally intervening in covert Rebel Group or criminal activities.
- Conduct research into advanced mana manipulation, spellcraft, and experimental applications beyond institutional limits.

### Internal Politics & Rivalries
- Organized into platoon-style units led by senior mages, typically drawn from the Magic Institutions (excluding Common Arcanum).
- Leaders coordinate operational and research priorities but rarely exercise political authority beyond their platoon.
- Rivalries arise over resource allocation, sanctioned expedition approval, and experimental methods; personal loyalty to the Royal Advisors often guides internal alignments.
- Occasional tension with other Magic Institutions occurs when institutional pride or protocol conflicts with citywide priorities.

### Culture and Expectations
- Highly disciplined, valuing precision, efficiency, and loyalty.
- Members cultivate both combat skill and magical expertise; research and fieldwork are both considered essential.
- Loyalty is generally to the Royal Advisors and the city’s stability, though individual ambition and philosophy influence behaviour.
- Publicly, they are professionals devoted to the Veil and city safety; internally, they debate theory, experiment, and tactical applications.

### Public Perception
- Feared for their combat ability and the flexibility to use magic offensively with legal immunity.
- Respected for their role in protecting Solasfall from the Fallows and ensuring safe exploration of the Hollowdeep.
- Mysterious due to their secretive operations, arcane experiments, and partial absence from public life.
