**Official Title:** Archmagus of the Runeweavers Institution  
**Role:** Master of runic and latent magic, overseeing creation of magical artefacts, runes, and circles. Trains students in precision, dexterity, and integration of Hollowdeep spells into practical magic.  
**Status:** Alive  
**Age:** 62 (pre-Veil Drenek; long-lived, experienced)  
**Condition:** Physically robust; occasional strain from long sessions of intricate runework. Mentally alert and meticulous.  
**Race:** Drenek  
**Languages:** Common, Stonechant, Solari

## Faction Relationships 
- **Royal Mages** – Technically reliable but slow; useful for sanctioned projects but often lacking finesse for precise runic integration.
- **Knights Orders** – Pragmatic utility in equipping soldiers with artefacts; values discipline and careful use of runes.
- **Other Magic Institutions** – Collegial respect; enjoys exchanging theories, though he finds some impractical or careless.
- **Royal Advisors** – Necessary bureaucratic interaction; must navigate carefully to secure approvals for artefact production.
- **General Public** – Largely irrelevant; views them as consumers or patrons of magical artefacts rather than active participants.
- **Explorers’ Guild & Hollowdeep Expeditions** – Highly valued; runes and artefacts are critical to explorer safety and mission efficiency.
- **Rebel Group** – Seen as a threat; prefers indirect monitoring only.
- **Cultists** – Dangerous; artefacts could be misused by them, so extreme caution is exercised.

## NPC Relationships
- **Archmagus Tyren Holl (Aetherbron Institution)** – Mutual professional respect; occasional debates on theory vs. application.
- **Magistra Lysandra Veilwright (Lifebinders Institution)** – Collaborates on enchanted artefacts for healing and expedition support.
- **Archmagus Vael Corrin (Riftconjurors Institution)** – Coordinates on rifts and runic stabilisation; values precision and reliability.

## Overview
Oren Valeclay is a formidable Drenek scholar whose long pre-Veil life has been dedicated to the mastery of runes and latent magic. His hands are almost as much a tool as his mind; years of delicate etching and arcane engineering have given him exceptional dexterity and insight.

He treats both students and colleagues with measured professionalism. His authority comes from decades of practical expertise rather than personal charisma. Oren is meticulous, cautious, and unafraid to critique less disciplined practitioners, though he fosters a culture of mentorship and innovation within the Runeweavers Institution.

He played a critical role in collaboration with the Emberwright Sanctum and the Order of the Gilded Chain to create artefacts used by the Veil and for Hollowdeep exploration.

## Appearance

Tall, muscular Drenek frame; scales a muted grey with darker striations along arms and back. Eyes deep amber, reflecting careful calculation and patience. Wears practical robes reinforced at joints for precision work, layered over functional armour for expeditions. Fingertips calloused and scarred from decades of rune etching. Typically carries a tool-laden satchel or staff inscribed with intricate runes.

## Traits
- **Runic Mastery:** Exceptional at creating, copying, and integrating magical circles.    
- **Artisan & Scholar:** Balances creativity with methodical precision; highly respected for craftsmanship.    
- **Cautious & Meticulous:** Rarely acts without careful assessment.   
- **Mentor:** Cultivates student skill through discipline and encouragement.   
- **Strategic Collaborator:** Recognizes the utility of allies and keeps dangerous factions at a distance.
