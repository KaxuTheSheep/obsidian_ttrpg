**Official Title:** Archmagus of the Aetherbron Institution  
**Role:** Reclusive master of raw and pathfinding magic; the guiding hand of the Aetherbron Institution and advisor to The Royal Mages. Known for unrivalled attunement to Mana, Tyren maintains institutional unity amid political pressures.  
**Status:** Alive  
**Age:** 58  
**Condition:** Physically healthy, though prone to long periods of isolation. Intense mana attunement leaves him occasionally disoriented in crowded or chaotic environments.
**Race:** Human  
**Languages:** Common, Solari, Emberglyphs

## Faction Relationships
- **The Royal Mages:** Trusted mentor and technical guide; occasionally conflicts with newer, more politically minded members.
- **The Knights Orders:** Respects their martial discipline but views their magical reliance as unsophisticated.
- **Other Magic Institutions:** Respected and feared; some younger heads envy his control over the Aetherbron’s prestige.
- **Royal Advisors:** Cooperative but distant; avoids politics where possible.
- **General Public:** Largely unaware of his influence; seen as an academic recluse.
- **The Explorers' Guild & Hollowdeep Expeditions:** Occasionally lends expertise in raw mana application for dangerous missions.

## NPC Relationships
- Lysandra Veilwright: Collaborates when healing or life-support magic interacts with raw mana constructs.
- **Vael Corrin:** Professional respect; differing philosophies on mana precision occasionally spark tension.
- **Headmasters of other institutions:** Respected as colleagues; correspondence mostly formal.

## Overview
Archmagus Tyren Holl rose to prominence for his unparalleled attunement to raw Mana. A former prodigy, he helped formalise much of the Aetherbron curriculum and contributed to the construction of the Veil. Tyren is a recluse by nature, spending weeks at a time in arcane study, yet his insight keeps the institution steady amid rivalries and the political machinations of the city.
Though rarely involved in public affairs, his opinions shape city-wide magical policy. Few can match his understanding of pathfinding magic, and his presence alone inspires confidence in students and faculty alike.

## Appearance
Tyren is a tall, wiry man with silver-streaked black hair and piercing grey eyes that seem to scan the flow of mana itself. He wears long, layered robes dyed in deep indigo and embroidered with faint, constantly shifting runic patterns that glow subtly in ambient mana. His hands are perpetually ink-stained from scribing raw mana sigils, and a faint aura of chilled energy surrounds him in still air.

## Traits
- **Mana Savant:** Deep attunement to raw and pathfinding magic, capable of feats beyond most mages.
- **Reclusive:** Avoids political entanglement, focusing on study and institutional stability.
- **Respectable Authority:** Commands loyalty and fear in equal measure among faculty and students.
- **Pragmatic Visionary:** Balances theoretical advancement with practical application, particularly in Veil maintenance.
- **Subtle Influence:** Operates behind the scenes to guide the magical hierarchy without overt control.
