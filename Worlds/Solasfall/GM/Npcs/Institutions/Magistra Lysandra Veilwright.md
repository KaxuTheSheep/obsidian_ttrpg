## Overview
Lysandra Veilwright is a celebrated master of Biomancy and Magical Healing. She rose to prominence through her work enhancing the Order of the Ashen Oath and supporting exploratory missions into the Hollowdeep. Her magical philosophy emphasizes the sanctity and resilience of life, and she has pioneered methods to stabilize injured mages and knights using Mana Stones and biomantic wards.

Lysandra also manages the Lifebinders Institution’s botanical and magical ecosystems, ensuring that both the city’s arcane and natural environments remain viable. Though deeply compassionate, she is strict in practical trials and enforces high standards among students and faculty alike.

## Appearance
Lysandra is of medium height with a strong, grounded posture. Her dark hair is streaked with silver, and her hands show faint bioluminescent traces from repeated biomantic work. She wears layered robes of muted green and gold, adorned with small sigils of life and protection. A Mana Stone-embedded amulet rests over her heart, linked to her life-enhancing spells. Her expression conveys calm authority, and her eyes often glow faintly when she channels mana for healing.

## Traits
- **Master Healer:** Capable of mending injuries and sustaining life even in extreme Hollowdeep conditions.
- **Protector of Life:** Upholds sanctity of life, extending her influence to citizens, knights, and explorers alike.
- **Meticulous Leader:** Enforces high standards in education, expeditions, and ritual practice.
- **Field Practitioner:** Regularly participates in hazardous missions to demonstrate leadership by example.
- **Respected Diplomat:** Bridges cooperation between Magic Institutions, The Knights Orders, and the The Explorers' Guild.
