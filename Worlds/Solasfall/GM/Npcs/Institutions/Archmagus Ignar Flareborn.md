**Official Title:** Archmagus of the Conflux  
**Role:** Master of elemental mana, specializing in fire, lightning, air, and earth manipulation. Oversees combat training, environmental conditioning, and elemental applications for city defense and Hollowdeep expeditions. Struggles with water magic, relying on collaborative support for aquatic operations.  
**Status:** Alive  
**Age:** 41  
**Condition:** Physically robust; occasional strain from prolonged mana combustion or high-intensity elemental work. Mentally sharp and theatrical.  
**Race:** Cindral  
**Languages:** Common, Emberglyphs, Solari, Flowtongue

## Overview
Ignar Flareborn is a charismatic, physically imposing Cindral whose mastery of fire, lightning, air, and earth makes him a spectacle in both combat and instruction. While water magic remains a weakness, he delegates or collaborates to ensure comprehensive elemental coverage. He is known for a performative approach — emphasizing display and dramatic precision — though his combat skill and tactical insight are formidable.

His influence helped make the Veil liveable, controlling elemental conditions like rain, temperature, and wind for city habitability. He values creativity and adaptability over rigid discipline, often clashing with the more meticulous Runeweavers Institution or Riftconjurors Institution members.

## Appearance
Tall and lean, with ember-like eyes and faintly glowing veins. Scaled skin reflects subtle flickers of heat, and his robes are vibrant, embroidered with elemental runes that shimmer during spellcasting. Hands are strong, calloused, and burn-resistant; small, controlled sparks often appear around his fingertips when idle.

## Traits
- **Elemental Expert:** Exceptional mastery over fire, lightning, air, and earth; water remains a challenge.
- **Performative & Combat-Oriented:** Prefers visible demonstrations of skill; highly effective in battle.
- **Innovative Strategist:** Values creative solutions over bureaucratic process.
- **Mentor:** Inspires students with spectacle and technical insight; demands courage under pressure.
- **Collaborative:** Relies on alliances with other institutions to overcome elemental weaknesses.
