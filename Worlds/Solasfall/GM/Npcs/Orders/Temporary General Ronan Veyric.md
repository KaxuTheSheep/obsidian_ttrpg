**Official Title:** Temporary General of the Iron Star  
**Role:** Oversees the defensive and protective duties of the Iron Star, ensuring walls, sacred grounds, and city exits remain secure. Directs knights using essences and divinity-enhanced abilities to maintain order and rapid response capability.  
**Status:** Alive  
**Age:** 38  
**Condition:** Physically robust; minor lingering strain from prolonged battlefield exposure.  
**Race:** Human  
**Languages:** Common, Solari

## Faction Relationships
- **The Royal Mages:** Respects their expertise in defensive magic, though prefers tangible battlefield utility over abstract theory.
- **The Knights Orders:** Cooperative with the Ashen Oath and Gilded Chain in shared operations; cautious of Veiled Watch tactics.
- **Other Magic Institutions:** Maintains professional distance, only consulting when defenses or expedition logistics require.
- **Royal Advisors:** Obliged to follow their directives; subtly resents interference in operational autonomy.
- **General Public:** Trusted as guardians; their presence reassures citizens at city borders and key positions.
- **The Explorers' Guild:** Values collaboration on high-risk Hollowdeep expeditions near rifts; Iron Star provides protection for safe ingress and egress.
- **The Rebel Group & Cultists:** Seen as threats to city safety; Iron Star deploys swiftly to suppress incursions.

## NPC Relationships
- **Archmagus Tyren Holl** – Occasionally coordinates defensive spellcraft around rifts.
- **Magistra Lysandra Veilwright** – Collaborates on fortifications and wards that sustain life-support for frontline knights.
- **General Kaelen Thorne** – Mutually respectful, though operational priorities sometimes clash; Ashen Oath’s zeal can frustrate measured defensive strategy.

## Overview
A former field commander elevated to temporary General, they embody the tension between duty and politics. Grounded in essences to enhance endurance and combat effectiveness, they are a pragmatic leader who values precision and reliability over spectacle. Cautious but decisive, they prioritize the protection of Solasfall and its outskirts while managing internal rivalries with other Orders. Their experience in the field grants them authority among the knights, though the absence of a permanent general leaves them navigating politics delicately.

## Appearance
Broad-shouldered and imposing, clad in reinforced armour inscribed with protective sigils and symbols of the Iron Star. Scars line forearms and shoulders, mementos of decades of frontline duty and rift expeditions. Wears a practical helm and gauntlets, favouring functionality over ornate embellishments. Eyes sharp and vigilant, constantly scanning for threats to the city.

## Traits
- **Battle-Hardened:** Extensive experience in frontline engagements and rift defence.
- **Pragmatic Commander:** Values efficiency, safety, and predictability over dramatic displays.
- **Essence-Enhanced:** Gains permanent boosts in strength, stamina, and resilience from Hollowdeep essences.
- **Political Savvy:** Navigates the complex dynamics of Solasfall’s Orders and Advisors.
- **Mentor:** Guides knights in disciplined defensive practices; ensures operational cohesion under pressure.
