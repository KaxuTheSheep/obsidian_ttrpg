Mana Overload Syndrome occurs when mana flow through a soul exceeds what the neurological structures generating it can withstand. The soul does not store mana — it conducts it. Under normal conditions the flow passes through and dissipates. Under extraordinary conditions the volume or excitation intensity of that flow becomes catastrophic, permanently damaging the neurological architecture the soul depends on.

## Cause
Two factors determine whether mana flow becomes lethal to the soul:

**Volume** is the primary cause. Forcing an extraordinary quantity of mana through a single soul — the kind required by civilisational scale magical undertakings — overwhelms the neurological structures regardless of excitation state. Normal casting, regardless of enhancement investment or failed control, does not approach this threshold. Backlash, Exhaustion, and Sanity damage are the consequences of ordinary magical failure. Mana Overload requires something far beyond that.

**Excitation intensity** acts as a multiplier. Highly excited mana exerts greater force on the neurological structures conducting it, producing the same catastrophic effect at lower volume than inert or weakly excited mana would require. This is the mechanism behind stone-related overloads — a highly excited Mana Stone failing or being forced against its excitation state releases a concentrated burst of intensely charged mana. A skilled mage has limited reaction time to withdraw before the spike passes through their soul.

The two factors compound. High volume of highly excited mana is the most dangerous combination possible, as demonstrated by the Veil's sealing ritual.

## Consequences
Mana Overload Syndrome is binary. The neurological structures either survive intact or they do not. There are no partial outcomes, no degrees of recovery, no gradual rehabilitation.

When overload occurs the consequences are permanent and total:
- All ability to conduct mana is lost. No casting of any kind is possible.
- All perception of mana is extinguished. The passive awareness through which mages experience ambient mana flow — the persistent background sense that defines a trained mage's relationship with the world — is gone entirely. Not diminished. Gone.
- Depending on the scale of the event, physical and neurological damage accompanies the loss. In extreme cases the damage extends beyond the soul's conducting function into broader neurological systems, leaving the mage alive but severely impaired in thought, memory, or independent function.

There is no recovery. The damage is structural and permanent.

## Stone Handling Risk
Mana Overload among practitioners most commonly occurs through catastrophic Mana Stone failure rather than casting. A stone that is damaged, rapidly depleted, or forced against its excitation state can fail unpredictably, releasing its charge in an uncontrolled burst through the soul of whoever is conducting mana through it at the time. Higher excitation intensity means higher risk — a weakly excited stone failing produces a manageable spike, while a highly excited stone failing can produce an overload before the practitioner can withdraw.

This is a known occupational hazard in the Explorers' Guild and the Magic Institutions. Handling protocols exist specifically to minimise forced excitation conflicts and to ensure practitioners withdraw from a stone showing structural instability before failure occurs.

## The King
The most prominent known case is the current King of Solasfall, who suffered catastrophic overload during the final sealing ritual of the Veil. The volume of mana conducted through a single soul during that moment was without precedent — the scale required to seal a structure encompassing the entire city in a single ritual act. The neurological damage extended beyond his conducting ability into broader function, leaving him alive but absent — incapable of governance or independent thought.

His condition is officially described as a severe illness. He rarely appears in public. When he does, magical means are used to present a convincing performance of health and presence. The Royal Advisors govern Solasfall entirely in his name. Few outside the innermost circle of power know the truth.
