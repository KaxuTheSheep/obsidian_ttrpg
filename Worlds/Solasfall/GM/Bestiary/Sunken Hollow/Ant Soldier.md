*Formica Rufa Cavensis, Soldier Caste*

Labrador sized, the soldier's mandibles alone look large enough to take a hand off. Its carapace has a darker, almost wet sheen compared to the workers, and acid hisses faintly where it beads along the mandible joints. It moves with none of the worker's nervous energy — slower, deliberate, certain.

Found throughout [[The Sunken Hollow]], soldiers defend the colony's aphid herds and respond to threats the [[Ant Worker|workers]] cannot handle alone.

## Behaviour
Soldiers respond to threats, not investigation. They move directly toward combat sounds, worker distress, or confirmed danger to the colony, without the worker's cautious approach. They prioritise the most immediate threat over the most vulnerable target and will not break off an engagement to retreat unless heavily outnumbered or the colony signal indicates withdrawal. They do not flank deliberately, but their numbers and the worker's harrying behaviour often create flanking incidentally.

## Ecosystem Notes
See [[The Sunken Hollow]] for the full ecosystem relationships. Soldiers defend [[Ant Worker|workers]] and aphid herds, hunt [[Cockroach|cockroaches]] opportunistically, and are themselves prey to the [[Mantis]] and [[Tarantula]] given the chance.
