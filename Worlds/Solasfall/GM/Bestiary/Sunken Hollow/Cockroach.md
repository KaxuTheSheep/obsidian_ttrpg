*Periplaneta Cavensis*

Stocky and low to the ground, roughly the mass of a large cat despite its compact build. Its carapace shimmers faintly with a translucent, glass-like sheen — a thin shell of conducted [[Mana|mana]] wrapped permanently around its body. When startled badly enough, that shell can leap free of its skin entirely.

Found scavenging throughout [[The Sunken Hollow]], usually alone or in small pairs rather than true colonies.

#Priorityaviour
Cockroaches are scavengers first, fighters only when cornered. They retreat toward gaps and crevices well before [[Blood]] loss becomes serious, attempting to squeeze through openings too small for pursuit. They do not coordinate with ants or other cockroaches and will flee past an ally without hesitation if it improves their own odds. A cockroach with no escape route fights only enough to create one.

Usually encountered in groups of one or two depending on conditions.

## Ecosystem Notes
See [[The Sunken Hollow]] for the full ecosystem relationships. Cockroaches scavenge opportunistically and are hunted by [[Ant Soldier|ant soldiers]], the [[Mantis]], and the [[Tarantula]] alike — they survive through avoidance rather than combat capability.
