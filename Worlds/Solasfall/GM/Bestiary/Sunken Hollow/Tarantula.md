*Theraphosa Cavensis*

Bear sized and low-slung, its legs span wider than a grown man's reach. Coarse hair bristles across its abdomen, faintly luminescent at the tips from years coating itself in the cave's bioluminescent spores. It does not see well. It does not need to.

The apex predator of [[The Sunken Hollow]] within the scope of its known ecosystem — though not a true apex of the wider [[Hollowdeep]]. It hunts from a burrow or concealed lair, and territory near such a lair is often marked by old webbing and prey remains.

## Vibration Sense (Passive)
The tarantula has poor eyesight and instead detects creatures through ground vibration. Against a creature moving at Walk pace or slower who has made no significant noise, the tarantula's Perception Tests are at **Disadvantage**. Against a creature Running, in active combat, or otherwise making significant noise, its Perception Tests are made normally, or at **Advantage** at GM discretion for particularly loud actions.

## Actions

### Fanged Bite
*Heavy curved fangs deliver a crushing, venomous bite.*
**Damage:** 1d10 + [[Attributes|Might]] [[Damage Types|Piercing]]
**Speed:** 8 + [[Attributes|Might]]
**Range:** +0.4m
**Type:** Melee
**On Hit:** Target makes a [[Attributes|Vitality]] Test (DN 13). On failure, gain **Disadvantage** on all Might and Grace Tests for 1d4 Rounds (paralytic venom — debilitating, not lethal).

### Directed Hairs
*Urticating hairs launched with mana-directed precision toward a chosen point.*
**Damage:** 1d4 [[Damage Types|Piercing]]
**Speed:** 9 + [[Attributes|Grace]]
**Optimal Range:** 8m
**Minimum Range:** 1m
**Type:** Ranged (Instant Projectile, no travel tracking — see [[Ranged Attacks]])
**On Hit:** Target makes a [[Attributes|Vitality]] Test (DN 11). On failure, gain **Disadvantage** on Perception Tests for 2 Rounds (eyes and mucous membranes irritated).

## Behaviour — Hunting
When actively hunting, the tarantula ambushes from its burrow or a concealed position, using surprise to commit directly to the Fanged Bite against the nearest or most isolated target. It does not warn or deter while hunting. If its initial target escapes melee range, it pursues briefly before switching to Directed Hairs against any target still within range, rather than abandoning the engagement immediately.

## Behaviour — Territorial
When not hunting, the tarantula prefers to avoid conflict. Creatures that stray near its territory without directly threatening it are watched but not engaged. If approached too closely or provoked, it first uses Directed Hairs as a warning at range. Only if this is ignored, if it takes damage, or if a creature enters melee range does it commit to the Fanged Bite and full engagement.

> **GM Note:** Set this creature's state — Hunting or Territorial — before running an encounter with it. The same stat block produces two very different encounters depending on which state is active.

## Ecosystem Notes
See [[The Sunken Hollow]] for the full ecosystem relationships. The tarantula opportunistically preys on everything in the Hollow's ecosystem, including [[Ant Worker|ants]], [[Cockroach|cockroaches]], and the [[Mantis]]. If weakened or killed, an [[Ant Soldier|ant colony]] may in turn opportunistically consume it.
