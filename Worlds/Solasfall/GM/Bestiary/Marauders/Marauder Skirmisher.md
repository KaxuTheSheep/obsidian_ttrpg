### Throwing Knife
**Damage:** 1d4+3 Cutting or Piercing
**Speed:** 15
**Thrown Range:** 10m
**Minimum Range:** 1m
**Type:** Thrown
**Traits:** Thrown, Close-Quarters Proficiency, Nimble, Concealable

## Trait — Won't Commit
Never engages in melee unless the target is already below 50% Blood; otherwise harries with thrown weapons and repositions each Round.

## Motive (roll or choose)
1–2: Debt/Weighing quota. 3–4: Starvation. 5–6: Revenge for a prior betrayal.

## Tell (Investigation DN 12)
Multiple small piercing wounds from different angles — the pattern doesn't match a single predator's attack style.
