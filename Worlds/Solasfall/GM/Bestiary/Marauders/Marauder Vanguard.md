*A person with a blade who ran out of better options.*

Every party knows the Weighing produces desperation before it produces corpses. What it doesn't advertise is how many of those corpses used to be someone else's Explorer.

## Trait — Cornered Desperation
Below 30% Blood, gain **+2** to Attack Tests. Cannot voluntarily Withdraw while this trait is active.

## Motive (roll or choose)
1–2: Debt/Weighing quota. 3–4: Starvation. 5–6: Revenge for a prior betrayal.

## Tell (Investigation DN 12)
Wounds are frantic and over-inflicted — inconsistent with a clean predator kill.
